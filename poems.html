Poems about GIS


Oh GIS, the tool of modern age,
That maps our world with grace and speed,
It weaves a tale of lands and sage,
And shows us patterns we must heed.
With data that flows like rivers wide,
It paints a picture of our earth,
From cities, forests, oceans tide,
To patterns that dictate our worth.
It tells us stories of human kin,
And all the choices we impart,
It shines a light on where we've been,
And helps us see with clearer heart.
Oh GIS, a bridge between art and math,
A tool of progress, a nurturer's path.
